Run To The Future

Run to the Future is an ambitious open world MMO survival game, blending dynamic exploration, strategic resource management, and player-driven combat. Players can scavenge, craft, and engage in thrilling encounters across a richly interactive environment. Status: In Development

Details / Features (Implemented by Me):
  • Item Collection & Inventory System: Seamless pickup, storage, and management of items with a clean, intuitive UI.
  • Persistent Datastores: Player progress and inventory are saved securely across sessions.
  • Dynamic Lighting System: Realistic day/night cycles and environmental lighting that enhance immersion.
  • MMO-Grade Combat System: Highly customizable combat mechanics designed for scalability in a multiplayer world.
